Privacy Policy for Club President

This Privacy Policy explains how Club President handles user information and how certain third-party services may process data when you use the app.

Effective date: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Club President ("the App") handles user information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Club President does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, videos, audio files, calendar data, health data, financial information, or precise location.

The App stores gameplay-related information locally on your device to support features such as save progress, career data, club data, player data, match data, and game settings.

The App may also use Google Play Games Services for features such as cloud save, achievements, and restoring gameplay progress. When these features are used, Google Play Games Services may process information such as your gamer identity, saved game progress, achievements, and related gameplay data.

The App may use Google Mobile Ads SDK / AdMob to show advertisements. This service may automatically collect or share data such as approximate location derived from IP address, app interactions, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance purposes.

2. How Information Is Used

Local gameplay data is used only to provide core game functionality, including saving and loading your progress, maintaining your in-game career, club information, and game settings.

Google Play Games Services data may be used to provide cloud save, achievements, restore functionality, analytics, diagnostics, security, and service improvements.

Advertising-related data may be used to show ads, measure ad performance, improve app and SDK performance, perform analytics, prevent fraud, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

Club President does not sell your personal data.

Some data may be shared with third-party service providers used by the App, including Google Play Games Services and Google Mobile Ads / AdMob, as necessary to provide gameplay, cloud save, achievements, advertising, analytics, diagnostics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance features.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK / AdMob

These services are provided by Google and are subject to Google's own privacy practices. Users can review Google’s Privacy Policy for more information about how Google handles data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Gameplay data may be stored locally on your device. If Google Play Games cloud save is used, save-related data may also be stored through Google Play Games Services.

Data transmitted by Google services is encrypted in transit. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been provided to the App in error, please contact us so we can review the issue.

7. Your Choices

You can remove locally stored gameplay data by uninstalling the App or clearing the App’s local storage through your device settings.

You can manage or reset advertising identifiers through your device settings where available. You can manage Google Play Games data through your Google Play Games profile or Google account settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
